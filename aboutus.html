About Fitness4Freaks

Helping Every Fitness Freak Reach Their Peak

Our Story

Founded by Calvin Matiza, Fitness4Freaks started with a simple mission: to make quality fitness products accessible to everyone in the UK. We believe that everyone deserves the tools to stay fit, healthy, and motivated, no matter their level of experience or budget.

Our vision is to create a community of fitness enthusiasts who inspire and support each other. From home workouts to professional training, Fitness4Freaks provides products that help you achieve your fitness goals without compromise.

We pride ourselves on trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. Each product in our store is carefully selected to meet our high standards, and our dropshipping model ensures you get the best prices with fast delivery.
